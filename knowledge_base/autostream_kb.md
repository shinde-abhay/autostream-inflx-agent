# AutoStream – Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream
AutoStream is a SaaS platform that provides **automated video editing tools** for content creators. Our AI-powered engine handles cutting, captioning, and exporting — so you can focus on creating.

---

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan – $29/month
- **10 videos per month**
- **720p resolution** output
- Basic auto-cut and trim
- Standard templates
- Email support (1–2 business day response)

### Pro Plan – $79/month
- **Unlimited videos per month**
- **4K resolution** output
- **AI-powered captions** (auto-generated)
- Advanced auto-editing with scene detection
- Custom brand templates
- **24/7 Priority Support**
- Analytics dashboard
- Multi-platform export presets (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, LinkedIn)
- Videos up to 4 hours long

## FAQ

**Q: What platforms can I export to?**
A: Pro plan supports YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, and LinkedIn. Basic supports YouTube and Instagram only.

**Q: Can I switch plans?**
A: Yes. Upgrades are immediate; downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Q: Is there a video length limit?**
A: Basic plan allows videos up to 30 minutes. Pro plan allows up to 4 hours.

**Q: Is AutoStream good for YouTube creators?**
A: Absolutely. The Pro plan's 4K export, unlimited videos, and AI captions are ideal for YouTube creators looking to scale their output.
